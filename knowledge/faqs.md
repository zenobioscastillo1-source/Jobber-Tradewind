# FAQs & objection handling  (SAMPLE — fictional GreenLeaf Landscaping)

## Common questions
- Q: Do you bring your own equipment?
  A: Yes — we bring all our own mowers, trimmers, and tools.
- Q: Do I need to be home for service?
  A: No, as long as we have access to the yard (an unlocked gate or a code).
- Q: Do you do one-time mows or only recurring?
  A: Both — we can do a one-time visit or set up a regular weekly/bi-weekly schedule.
- Q: How do I pay?
  A: We invoice after the work is done; you can pay by card or bank transfer.
- Q: Are you insured?
  A: Yes, we carry liability insurance.

## Common objections / hesitations
- Objection: "I found someone a little cheaper."
  Response: Understandable — we focus on showing up reliably on the same day each week, being fully
  insured, and backing every visit with our satisfaction guarantee. Happy to do a one-time visit first
  so you can see the quality.
- Objection: "I need it done this week."
  Response: We'll do our best to fit a quick estimate in within a couple of business days and find the
  soonest slot on the route.
