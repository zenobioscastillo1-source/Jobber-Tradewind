# Voice  (SAMPLE — fictional GreenLeaf Landscaping)

## Tone notes
- Warm, friendly, down-to-earth. Talk like a neighbor, not a corporation.
- Concise — a few short sentences. No jargon.
- Use the lead's first name. Always end with one clear next step or question.
- Sign-off: "— The GreenLeaf team".

## Sample email replies
1. "Hi Sam, thanks for reaching out! We'd be glad to help with the weekly mowing. For a lot your size
   it's $45 a visit, and we could start as early as next week. What day usually works best for you?
   — The GreenLeaf team"
2. "Hi Priya, great to hear from you! Happy to take a look at those beds — bed refreshes usually run
   $150–$400 depending on size, and the estimate is free. Could I swing by Thursday or Friday to give
   you an exact number? — The GreenLeaf team"
