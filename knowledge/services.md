# Services  (SAMPLE — fictional GreenLeaf Landscaping)

## Services offered
- **Lawn mowing & edging** — weekly or bi-weekly recurring service; includes mowing, edging, and blowing clippings off walks/drive.
- **Garden bed care** — weeding, cleanup, and refreshing existing beds.
- **Mulch installation** — supply and spread mulch in beds.
- **Seasonal planting & bed design** — selecting and planting annuals/perennials and laying out new beds.
- **Shrub & hedge trimming** — shaping and tidying shrubs and hedges.
- **Irrigation check & minor repair** — inspecting sprinkler zones and fixing/replacing heads.
- **Seasonal yard cleanup** — spring and fall cleanups (leaves, debris, cut-back).

## Explicitly NOT offered
- **Large tree removal or tree climbing** — we refer these to a licensed arborist.
- **Hardscaping** (concrete, paver patios, retaining walls) — not a service we provide.
- **Chemical lawn/pest treatment** — refer to a licensed applicator.
- **Artificial turf installation** — not offered.
- **Snow removal** — not offered.
